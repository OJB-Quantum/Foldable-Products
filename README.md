# Foldable-Products
A repository that compiles data on interesting foldable products available on the market. Made by Onri Jay Benally.

---

I think foldable and collapsible products are not only fun, but can have a lot of utility in everyday use cases. Their value comes from their reversible compacting transformation. One thing I like about foldable products is their storage efficiency as they often do not take up much volume per unit. Another is that they are often easy to transport and can sometimes be made of compliant mechanisms (meaning that they can be made of a very few parts, and sometimes monolithic in design).
