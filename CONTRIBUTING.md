# Contributing

Add one product per row and include at least one source URL.

## Required fields

- product_id
- brand
- product_name
- market_category
- transformation_family
- transformation_subtype
- fold_mechanism
- fold_architecture
- source_url
- source_type
- review_status

## Evidence expectations

Use higher evidence scores for official product pages, standards documents,
peer-reviewed papers, teardown reports, and direct measurements. Use lower
scores for marketplace listings, social posts, and unsupported claims.

## Suggested measurements

Add folded dimensions, deployed dimensions, mass, materials, hinge cycle claims,
certifications, release year, price, region, availability, and transformation
mechanism notes when available.
